# Routine 4 - Lower Body Stability

**Theme:** Prepare knees, ankles, hips, and stances.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Butt Kicks](/exercises/butt-kicks/) (30 seconds)
2. [Squat with Rising Block](/exercises/squat-rising-block/) (10 reps)

### 2. Joint Mobility
3. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
4. [Knee Circles](/exercises/knee-circles/)
5. [Ankle Circles](/exercises/ankle-circles/)
6. [Trunk Twist](/exercises/trunk-twists/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
7. [Reverse Lunges](/exercises/reverse-lunges/) (8 reps per side)
8. [Bodyweight Squats](/exercises/bodyweight-squats/) (10 reps)
9. [Standing Calf Raises](/exercises/standing-calf-raises/) (12 reps)

### 4. Karate Integration
10. [Horse Stance Weight Shifts](/exercises/horse-stance-weight-shifts/) (30 seconds)
11. [Front Stance Transitions](/exercises/front-stance-transitions/) (8 reps per side)

### 5. Finish / Reset
12. [Standing Calf Stretch](/exercises/standing-calf-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
13. [Standing Hip Flexor/Quad Stretch](/exercises/standing-quad-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
14. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
