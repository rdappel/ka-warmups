# Routine 4 - Lower Body Stability

**Theme:** Prepare knees, ankles, hips, and stances.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Butt Kicks](/exercises/butt-kicks/) (30 seconds)
2. [Squat with High Block](/exercises/squat-high-block/) (10 reps)
3. [March with Knee Hold](/exercises/march-with-knee-hold/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
4. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
5. [Knee Circles](/exercises/knee-circles/)
6. [Ankle Circles](/exercises/ankle-circles/)
7. [Trunk Twist](/exercises/trunk-twists/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
8. [Reverse Lunges](/exercises/reverse-lunges/) (8 reps per side)
9. [Single-Leg Balance Reaches](/exercises/single-leg-balance-reaches/) (8 reps per side)
10. [Standing Calf Raises](/exercises/standing-calf-raises/) (12 reps)

### 4. Karate Integration
11. [Horse Stance Weight Shifts](/exercises/horse-stance-weight-shifts/) (30 seconds)
12. [Front Stance Transitions](/exercises/front-stance-transitions/) (8 reps per side)
13. [Sprawl Return to Fighting Stance](/exercises/sprawl-fighting-stance/) (8 reps)

### 5. Finish / Reset
14. [Standing Calf Stretch](/exercises/standing-calf-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
15. [Standing Hip Flexor/Quad Stretch](/exercises/standing-hip-flexor-quad-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
16. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
