# Routine 9 - Recovery Day

**Theme:** Light movement for days before tests, graduations, tournaments, or heavy training.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [March in Place](/exercises/march-in-place/) (30 seconds)
2. [Step Jack into Defensive Stance](/exercises/step-jack-defensive-stance/) (30 seconds)
3. [Jog in Place](/exercises/jog-in-place/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
4. [Neck Turns and Nods](/exercises/neck-turns-and-nods/)
5. [Shoulder Circles](/exercises/shoulder-circles/)
6. [Wrist Circles](/exercises/wrist-circles/)
7. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
8. [Standing Cross-Body Reaches](/exercises/standing-cross-body-reaches/) (8 reps per side)
9. [Front-to-Back Leg Swings](/exercises/front-to-back-leg-swings/) (8 reps per side)
10. [Elephant Walks](/exercises/elephant-walks/) (8 reps)

### 4. Karate Integration
11. [Slow Step with Straight Punch](/exercises/slow-step-straight-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)
12. [Slow Front Kick](/exercises/slow-front-kick/) (8 reps per side)
13. [Slow Low Block in Front Stance](/exercises/slow-low-block-front-stance/) (8 reps per side)

### 5. Finish / Reset
14. [Standing Hamstring Stretch](/exercises/standing-hamstring-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
15. [Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch](/exercises/cross-body-shoulder-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
16. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
