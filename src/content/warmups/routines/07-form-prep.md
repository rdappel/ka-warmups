# Routine 7 - Form Prep

**Theme:** Prepare posture, stances, control, and deliberate technique.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Jog in Place](/exercises/jog-in-place/) (30 seconds)
2. [Jumping Jack with Jab](/exercises/jumping-jack-jab/) (30 seconds)
3. [March in Place](/exercises/march-in-place/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
4. [Shoulder Circles](/exercises/shoulder-circles/)
5. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
6. [Knee Circles](/exercises/knee-circles/)
7. [Ankle Circles](/exercises/ankle-circles/)
8. [Trunk Twist](/exercises/trunk-twists/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
9. [Front Stance Transitions](/exercises/front-stance-transitions/) (8 reps per side)
10. [Standing Knee Hugs](/exercises/standing-knee-hugs/) (8 reps per side)
11. [Standing Hip Openers](/exercises/standing-hip-openers/) (8 reps per side)

### 4. Karate Integration
12. [Slow Low Block in Front Stance](/exercises/slow-low-block-front-stance/) (8 reps per side)
13. [Slow Front Kick](/exercises/slow-front-kick/) (8 reps per side)
14. [Slow Step with Straight Punch](/exercises/slow-step-straight-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)

### 5. Finish / Reset
15. [Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch](/exercises/cross-body-shoulder-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
16. [Standing Calf Stretch](/exercises/standing-calf-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
17. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
