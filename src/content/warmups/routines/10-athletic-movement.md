# Routine 10 - Athletic Movement

**Theme:** Energetic full-body movement without excessive fatigue.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Burpees](/exercises/burpees/) (8 reps)
2. [Jumping Jack with Jab](/exercises/jumping-jack-jab/) (30 seconds)
3. [High Knees](/exercises/high-knees/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
4. [Shoulder Circles](/exercises/shoulder-circles/)
5. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
6. [Knee Circles](/exercises/knee-circles/)
7. [Ankle Circles](/exercises/ankle-circles/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
8. [Inchworm Walkouts](/exercises/inchworm-walkouts/) (6 reps)
9. [Skater Hops](/exercises/skater-hops/) (10 reps per side)
10. [Squat Jumps](/exercises/squat-jumps/) (10 reps)

### 4. Karate Integration
11. [Sprawl Return to Fighting Stance](/exercises/sprawl-fighting-stance/) (8 reps)
12. [Front Stance Step with Cross](/exercises/front-stance-step-reverse-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)
13. [Single-Leg Balance with Front Kick](/exercises/single-leg-balance-front-kick/) (8 reps per side)

### 5. Finish / Reset
14. [Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch](/exercises/cross-body-shoulder-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
15. [Standing Calf Stretch](/exercises/standing-calf-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
16. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
