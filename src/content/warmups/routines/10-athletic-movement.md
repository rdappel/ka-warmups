# Routine 10 - Athletic Movement

**Theme:** Energetic full-body movement without excessive fatigue.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Burpees](/exercises/burpees/) (8 reps)
2. [Jumping Jack with Jab](/exercises/jumping-jack-jab/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
3. [Shoulder Circles](/exercises/shoulder-circles/)
4. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
5. [Knee & Ankle Circles](/exercises/knee-ankle-circles/)
6. [Wrist Circles](/exercises/wrist-circles/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
7. [Mountain Climbers](/exercises/mountain-climbers/) (20 seconds)
8. [Skater Hops](/exercises/skater-hops/) (10 reps per side)
9. [Squat Jumps](/exercises/squat-jumps/) (10 reps)

### 4. Karate Integration
10. [Sprawl Return to Fighting Stance](/exercises/sprawl-fighting-stance/) (8 reps)
11. [Front Stance Step with Cross](/exercises/front-stance-step-reverse-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)

### 5. Finish / Reset
12. [Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch](/exercises/cross-body-shoulder-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
13. [Standing Calf Stretch](/exercises/standing-calf-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
14. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
