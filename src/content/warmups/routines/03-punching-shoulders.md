# Routine 3 - Punching & Shoulders

**Theme:** Warm the upper body and reinforce safe striking mechanics.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Light Shadow Boxing](/exercises/light-shadow-boxing/) (30 seconds)
2. [Jumping Jack with Double Jab](/exercises/jumping-jack-double-jab/) (30 seconds)
3. [Fast Feet](/exercises/fast-feet/) (20 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
4. [Shoulder Circles](/exercises/shoulder-circles/)
5. [Arm Crosses](/exercises/arm-crosses/)
6. [Wrist Circles](/exercises/wrist-circles/)
7. [Trunk Twist (Add Punch)](/exercises/trunk-twist-add-punch/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
8. [Inchworm Walkouts](/exercises/inchworm-walkouts/) (6 reps)
9. [Scapular Push-Ups](/exercises/scapular-push-ups/) (10 reps)
10. [Punch-Outs](/exercises/punch-outs/) (10 reps, alternating sides)

### 4. Karate Integration
11. [Step-In Jab, Cross](/exercises/step-in-jab-reverse-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)
12. [Horse Stance Double Punch](/exercises/horse-stance-double-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)
13. [Slow Low Block in Front Stance](/exercises/slow-low-block-front-stance/) (8 reps per side)

### 5. Finish / Reset
14. [Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch](/exercises/cross-body-shoulder-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
15. [Over the Top Tricep Stretch](/exercises/over-the-top-tricep-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
16. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
