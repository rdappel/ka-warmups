# Routine 3 - Punching & Shoulders

**Theme:** Warm the upper body and reinforce safe striking mechanics.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Light Shadow Boxing](/exercises/light-shadow-boxing/) (30 seconds)
2. [Jumping Jack with Double Jab](/exercises/jumping-jack-double-jab/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
3. [Shoulder Circles](/exercises/shoulder-circles/)
4. [Arm Swings Across Chest](/exercises/arm-swings-across-chest/) (10 reps)
5. [Wrist Circles](/exercises/wrist-circles/)
6. [Trunk Twist (Add Punch)](/exercises/trunk-twist-add-punch/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
7. [Inchworm Walkouts](/exercises/inchworm-walkouts/) (6 reps)
8. [Scapular Push-Ups](/exercises/scapular-push-ups/) (10 reps)
9. [Standing Cross-Body Reaches](/exercises/standing-cross-body-reaches/) (8 reps per side)

### 4. Karate Integration
10. [Horse Stance Double Punch](/exercises/horse-stance-double-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)
11. [Step-In Jab, Cross](/exercises/step-in-jab-reverse-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)

### 5. Finish / Reset
12. [Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch](/exercises/cross-body-shoulder-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
13. [Over the Top Tricep Stretch](/exercises/over-the-top-tricep-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
14. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
