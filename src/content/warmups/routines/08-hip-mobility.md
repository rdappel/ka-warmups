# Routine 8 - Hip Mobility

**Theme:** Improve hip range of motion for stances and kicks.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Side Shuffle](/exercises/side-shuffle/) (30 seconds)
2. [Jump Rope Simulation](/exercises/jump-rope-simulation/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
3. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
4. [Trunk Twist](/exercises/trunk-twists/)
5. [Knee Circles](/exercises/knee-circles/)
6. [Ankle Circles](/exercises/ankle-circles/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
7. [World's Greatest Stretch](/exercises/world-s-greatest-stretch/) (5 reps per side)
8. [Standing Hip Openers](/exercises/standing-hip-openers/) (8 reps per side)
9. [Butterfly Pulses](/exercises/butterfly-pulses/) (15 reps)

### 4. Karate Integration
10. [Side Kick Chamber Hold](/exercises/side-kick-chamber-holds/) (8 reps per side)
11. [Slow Front Kick to Reverse Side Kick](/exercises/slow-front-kick-to-reverse-side-kick/) (8 reps per side)

### 5. Finish / Reset
12. [Butterfly Stretch](/exercises/butterfly-stretch/) (30 seconds)
13. [Standing Hamstring Stretch](/exercises/standing-hamstring-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
14. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
