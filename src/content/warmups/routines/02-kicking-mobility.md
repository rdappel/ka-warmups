# Routine 2 - Kicking Mobility

**Theme:** Open the hips and prepare for controlled kicking.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [High Knees](/exercises/high-knees/) (30 seconds)
2. [Butt Kicks](/exercises/butt-kicks/) (30 seconds)
3. [Seal Jack Jab-Cross Combo](/exercises/seal-jack-jab-cross-combo/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
4. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
5. [Knee Circles](/exercises/knee-circles/)
6. [Ankle Circles](/exercises/ankle-circles/)
7. [Standing Hip Flexor/Quad Stretch](/exercises/standing-hip-flexor-quad-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
8. [Cossack Squats](/exercises/cossack-squats/) (8 reps per side)
9. [Side-to-Side Leg Swings](/exercises/side-leg-swings/) (8 reps per side)
10. [Reverse Lunges with Torso Rotation](/exercises/reverse-lunges-with-torso-rotation/) (8 reps per side)

### 4. Karate Integration
11. [Slow Front Kick](/exercises/slow-front-kick/) (8 reps per side)
12. [Slow Hook-Round Kick](/exercises/slow-hook-round-kick/) (8 reps per side)
13. [Chamber Holds](/exercises/chamber-holds/) (8 reps per side)

### 5. Finish / Reset
14. [Standing Hamstring Stretch](/exercises/standing-hamstring-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
15. [Butterfly Stretch](/exercises/butterfly-stretch/) (30 seconds)
16. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
