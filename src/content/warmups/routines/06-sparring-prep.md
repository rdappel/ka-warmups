# Routine 6 - Sparring Prep

**Theme:** Prepare footwork, reaction, and light movement.

## Overview

### 1. Raise Body Temperature
1. [Fast Feet](/exercises/fast-feet/) (20 seconds)
2. [Shuffle with Jab](/exercises/shuffle-jab/) (30 seconds)

### 2. Joint Mobility
3. [Shoulder Circles](/exercises/shoulder-circles/)
4. [Wrist Circles](/exercises/wrist-circles/)
5. [Hip Circles](/exercises/hip-circles/)
6. [Ankle Circles](/exercises/ankle-circles/)

### 3. Dynamic Mobility & Activation
7. [Skater Hops](/exercises/skater-hops/) (10 reps per side)
8. [Walking Lunges](/exercises/walking-lunges/) (8 reps per side)
9. [Lateral Shuffle](/exercises/lateral-shuffle/) (30 seconds)

### 4. Karate Integration
10. [Forward-and-Back Shuffle with Guard](/exercises/forward-and-back-shuffle-with-guard/) (30 seconds)
11. [Step Off-Line with Cross](/exercises/step-off-line-reverse-punch/) (10 reps, alternating sides)

### 5. Finish / Reset
12. [Standing Calf Stretch](/exercises/standing-calf-stretch/) (30 seconds per side)
13. [Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch](/exercises/cross-body-shoulder-stretch/) (30 seconds, alternating sides)
14. [Stretch Tight Areas](/exercises/stretch-tight-areas/)
