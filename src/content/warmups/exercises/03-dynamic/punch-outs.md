---
title: "Punch-Outs"
---

Starting in a defensive stance, throw alternate punches (jab, cross) in a controlled manner. Instructors can cue to change puch types (hook, uppercut), but should return to "straight punches" each time. Keep the guard up and the feet under control. Do not cross the feet.

**Modification:** Slow the pace or shorten the extension if the shoulders feel tight.
